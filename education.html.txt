<h1><b>ACADEMIC QUALIFICATION</b></h1>
Academic qualifications refer to certifications awarded to students upon successful completion of a course of study in higher education, typically at a college or university. Such educational institutions commonly offer degrees at various levels, including Bachelor's, Master's, and Doctorate.
